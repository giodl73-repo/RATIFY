# Institution Record Contract

Each comparative record must include:

- jurisdiction and governmental level;
- mechanism type;
- who may initiate it;
- signature or agenda threshold;
- voting and turnout requirements;
- whether the result is binding;
- subject-matter exclusions;
- review by courts or other institutions;
- implementation authority;
- observed participation and outcomes;
- documented benefits, failures, and distributional concerns;
- primary sources and observation date; and
- transfer limits for U.S. use.

Mechanism types initially include citizen initiative, legislative referendum,
mandatory referendum, repeal or abrogative referendum, recall, citizen
assembly, participatory budgeting, and program sunset or reauthorization.
