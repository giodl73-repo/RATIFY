# Binary Ballot Readiness

## Current decision

**Not ready for Yes/No ratification.**

The bridge now has a traceable fiscal target, a complete synthetic ballot, an
actual-ballot aggregation method, a zero unsupported-savings rule, and specific
candidate instruments. It does not yet have selected statutory variants,
legislative text, a joint current-baseline score, quantified distribution,
rights and service floors, or an implementation plan.

## Illustrative shape only

When those gates close, the ballot can take this form:

> Shall the United States adopt the Primary Balance Transition Package,
> consisting of the attached specified revenue and primary-outlay laws,
> including all household protections and implementation provisions, with the
> jointly scored fiscal and debt effects shown below?

```text
[ ] YES — adopt the complete package and funding
[ ] NO  — retain current law and its projected debt path
```

The phrase “attached specified laws” cannot be replaced by funding categories
or isolated option scores.
