# Joint Scoring Request

The synthetic medoid supplies a fiscal envelope, not legislation. Before the
challenger can face current law, analysts need one integrated specification and
score.

## Instrument decisions required

### Progressive revenue

Choose the AGI surtax threshold and rate, capital-gains treatment, and whether a
financial-transactions tax remains in the package. Define filing, indexing,
avoidance, retirement-account, and interaction rules.

### Broad revenue

Choose an emissions-tax base and trajectory with household rebates and regional
transition, or specify a VAT rate, base, exemptions, border treatment, and
refundable household protection. A 5% VAT is larger than this category's
current envelope and cannot simply be scaled linearly without rescoring.

### Tax expenditures

Choose the employer-health exclusion cap and itemized-deduction variant. Model
geography, age, health risk, wages, charitable giving, housing, state taxes, and
interactions with the surtax and other income-tax changes.

### Primary outlays

Replace an across-the-board defense number with a capability, force, workforce,
contract, and regional transition plan. Specify Medicare Advantage benchmark,
risk-adjustment, quality, supplemental-benefit, network, county, and enrollee
transition rules.

### Administration

Score zero. Add only program-specific, evidence-backed net savings after
implementation cost, false-positive risk, recoveries, displacement, and timing.

## Required integrated outputs

- Annual 2026–2036 revenue, primary outlay, implementation, and interest effects.
- Current-law and package debt paths on the same February 2026 CBO basis.
- Overlap, behavioral, macroeconomic, and timing effects.
- Household incidence by income, age, family type, and relevant demographic groups.
- State, regional, industry, employer, provider, worker, and beneficiary effects.
- Health access and quality, defense readiness, and essential-service floors.
- Legal authority, legislative text, administrative capacity, and effective dates.
- Forecast uncertainty, reserve, automatic-correction corridor, and early-review triggers.
- Current-law comparison and a plain-language Yes/No ballot record.

Until those outputs exist, the package is a research shortlist—not an offer to
voters.
