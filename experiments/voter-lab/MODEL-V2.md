# Voter Lab V2

V2 retains the 11 synthetic mixture components and 14 bounded traits from V1,
but samples within-component variation through four latent factors plus explicit
residual variance. It also enables random card order and fatigue-scaled
second-card information decay in the comprehension bridge.

Validation signs in the retained validation mixture:

- numeracy / strategic sophistication: +0.36;
- fiscal concern / pocketbook sensitivity: +0.26;
- institutional trust / anti-establishment: −0.36;
- social susceptibility / issue concentration: +0.22; and
- numeracy / fatigue sensitivity: −0.20.

These are generated consequences of synthetic loadings, not empirical facts.
V2 is promoted for internal experiments because it passes its declared
invariants and bridge criteria. It is not calibrated public opinion.
