# Future Citizen

## Key question

*Can a person arriving years later understand what was decided, why, with what evidence, and how to challenge or revise it?*

Require durable plain-language records, visible authority, versioned evidence,
implementation reports, dissent, expiration rules, and a usable correction or
appeal path.
