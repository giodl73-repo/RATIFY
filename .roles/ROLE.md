# RATIFY Role Index

RATIFY uses AI-simulated review lenses to challenge its evidence, designs, and
experiments. They are not claims of real-person or professional review.

## Parliament

| File | Voice | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `parliament/comparative-evidence-steward.md` | Comparative Evidence Steward | Transferable lesson vs. institutional cherry-picking |
| `parliament/constitutional-boundary-reviewer.md` | Constitutional Boundary Reviewer | Democratic innovation vs. lawful authority and rights |
| `parliament/democratic-legitimacy-reviewer.md` | Democratic Legitimacy Reviewer | Durable consensus vs. unequal voice and minority veto |
| `parliament/minority-rights-guardian.md` | Minority Rights Guardian | Majority rule vs. protected rights and vulnerable groups |
| `parliament/deliberation-information-steward.md` | Deliberation and Information Steward | Accessible ballots vs. manipulation and overload |
| `parliament/simulation-auditor.md` | Simulation Auditor | Useful exploration vs. synthetic certainty |
| `parliament/innovation-scout.md` | Innovation Scout | Learning and growth vs. novelty without evidence |

## Stakeholders

| File | Stakeholder | Primary concern |
|---|---|---|
| `stakeholders/election-administrator.md` | Election Administrator | Can the mechanism be administered, audited, and certified? |
| `stakeholders/future-citizen.md` | Future Citizen | Can an ordinary voter understand power, choices, effects, and recourse? |

## Review order

1. Evidence packets: Comparative Evidence Steward, then Innovation Scout.
2. U.S. designs: Constitutional Boundary, Democratic Legitimacy, Minority Rights.
3. Ballots and public materials: Deliberation and Information, Future Citizen.
4. Experiments: Simulation Auditor plus the roles implicated by the rule tested.
5. Operational proposals: Election Administrator before readiness claims.
