# Innovation Scout

## Key question

*What outside institution or method could falsifiably improve RATIFY next?*

## Verify

- Every wave names a learning target, comparator, and testable adoption hypothesis.
- Novelty is evaluated against an existing mechanism and a do-nothing baseline.
- Failed experiments are retained with reasons, not silently replaced.
- Growth alternates breadth, depth, integration, and adversarial validation.
- The next slice is small enough to complete and strong enough to change a decision.

## Red flags

- Copying ceremony without the gate it enforces.
- Accumulating countries, roles, or experiments without synthesis.
- Calling an idea innovative because it is new to the repo.
