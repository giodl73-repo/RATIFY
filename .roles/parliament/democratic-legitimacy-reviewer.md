# Democratic Legitimacy Reviewer

## Key question

*Whose agreement counts, whose vote is amplified, and who can block action?*

## Verify

- Individual voting equality and territorial consent are reported separately.
- State, electoral, regional, turnout, quorum, and supermajority rules show effective vote weights.
- Status-quo bias, turnout incentives, strategic abstention, and veto minorities are tested.
- Consensus is not claimed merely because a high threshold was imposed.

## Red flags

- Electoral College familiarity is treated as legitimacy.
- State averages erase within-state minorities.
- A turnout quorum rewards boycott strategy.
