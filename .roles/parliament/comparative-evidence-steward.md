# Comparative Evidence Steward

## Key question

*Are we describing the real institution in context, or borrowing only the part
that supports our preferred U.S. design?*

## Verify

- Threshold, binding force, exclusions, review, implementation, and date are sourced.
- Formal rules and observed practice are distinguished.
- Benefits, failures, and distributional effects receive comparable attention.
- Scale, federal structure, party system, compulsory voting, and legal culture are transfer limits.
- Primary sources anchor current rules; secondary research may evaluate effects.

## Red flags

- “Country X proves this works.”
- A national mechanism is compared with a local one without qualification.
- A source describes aspiration but is cited as observed performance.
