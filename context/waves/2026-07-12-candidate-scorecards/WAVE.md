# Wave: Candidate Scorecards and Comprehension

## Objective

Create parallel, audit-ready scorecard packets for the progressive primary-
balance and protected partial-path candidates, then test whether synthetic
voters with varied numeracy, fatigue, trust, and pocketbook sensitivity can
recover the packages' actual differences without being steered toward either.

## Guardrail

This wave tests information recovery, confusion, and framing sensitivity. It
does not estimate support while fiscal and distributional results remain
synthetic.

## Pulses

| Pulse | Purpose | Status |

| 01 | Build symmetric machine-readable scorecards | active |

## Decision gate

Can differently situated synthetic readers identify who pays, the fiscal target,
the debt tradeoff, uncertainty, and correction rule without systematic framing
advantage or overload?
