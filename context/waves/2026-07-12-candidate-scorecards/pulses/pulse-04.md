# Pulse 04: Revision and External-Review Decision

## Decision

Advance both candidates as symmetric external-review packets using the layered
card and fact check. Do not advance either to a public binding ballot.

## Required external work

- independent joint fiscal, distributional, service, legal, and implementation
  scoring;
- human comprehension testing with card order randomized;
- oversampling and accessibility testing for low numeracy, language access,
  disability, high fatigue, and limited time;
- qualitative interviews on the debt and correction-corridor language; and
- publication of failures without tuning the test to favor a candidate.

## Role findings

- Deliberation: equal formatting alone failed; layered comparison and active
  fact recovery are the retained hypothesis.
- Future citizen: common policy, changed field, uncertainty, and correction
  authority remain visible and versioned.
- Simulation audit: pass for internal sensitivity, fail as human validation.
- Election administration: the fact check may educate but may not condition a
  citizen's legal right to vote or invalidate a ballot.
