# Pulse 03: Comprehension and Framing Stress

## Result

Five seeds with 10,000 synthetic agents per run produced:

| Presentation | Progressive | Partial path |
|---|---:|---:|
| Neutral, low fatigue | 56.1% | 55.9% |
| Neutral, high fatigue | 49.6% | 49.6% |
| Layered plain language | 63.9% | 63.9% |
| Layered with fact check | 72.2% | 72.0% |
| Overloaded | 34.4% | 34.9% |

Neutral symmetry passes mechanically: card gaps are 0.16 and 0.03 percentage
points. Candidate-favorable framing reduces recovery of the favored card's
obscured cost field by roughly five points relative to the other card.

## Boundary

The model assigns heuristic information-recovery effects to layering and fact
checks. The improvement is therefore a falsifiable interface hypothesis, not
observed human comprehension. No support or turnout is generated. Trait
correlations and order effects remain disabled.
