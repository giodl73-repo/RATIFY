# Pulse 01: Symmetric Scorecard Contract

## Result

Created one machine-readable record with a shared policy block, nine identical
comparison fields, common baselines, equal warnings, and a card-order
randomization rule.

## Information contract

Each card states its marginal change, exposed groups, fiscal target, central and
lower synthetic results, 2036 debt, correction corridor, action outside the
corridor, and unscored status. The common package appears once so repetition
does not create differential fatigue.
