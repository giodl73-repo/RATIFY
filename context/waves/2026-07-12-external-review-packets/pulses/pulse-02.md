# Pulse 02: Nonfiscal and Legal Review

## Result

Specified parallel service, affected-group, implementation, authority, remedy,
and rights questions. The review must separate ordinary statutory policy from
any self-executing national voter chamber that would require constitutional
change under Article I lawmaking rules.

A fiscal pass cannot waive a capability, access, continuity, authority, rights,
or implementation failure. Reviewers must say whether a failure requires
revision, financing, delay, or rejection.
