# Pulse 03: Human Comprehension Protocol

## Result

Specified preregistration, randomized candidate order, flat-versus-layered
presentation, slanted and overloaded stress conditions, a minimum initial
sample of 400 completions, accessibility reporting, open-text explanations,
and a non-gating educational fact check.

Provisional acceptance requires at least 70% recovery of every primary fact
overall, 60% in adequately sampled information and accessibility strata, no
more than five points of neutral card or order asymmetry, fewer than 10%
confidently treating synthetic figures as official, and no material
accessibility defect.
