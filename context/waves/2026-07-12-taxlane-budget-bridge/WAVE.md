# Wave: TAXLANE Budget Bridge

## Objective

Generalize TAXLANE's annual 100-point budget-ballot experiment into a RATIFY
package-construction experiment in which every ballot also closes funding, then
produce one reviewed challenger for binary ratification.

## Allowed scope

- Preserve TAXLANE source and experiment provenance.
- Copy or adapt only what RATIFY needs; TAXLANE remains the fiscal evidence owner.
- Add funding, borrowing, debt, aggregation, and binary-ratification layers.
- Keep every output synthetic and distinguish package construction from law.
- Do not claim constitutional authority or measured public preferences.
