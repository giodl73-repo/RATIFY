# Pulse 02: Balanced Use-and-Funding Ballots

## Result

Generated 6,000 synthetic complete ballots. Each ballot contains:

- a 15-lane use allocation summing to 100;
- a fiscal path choice;
- five adjustment-funding shares summing to 100; and
- a hard cap limiting verified administrative savings to 5% of the adjustment.

The fiscal path and funding mix are linked. Allocation shares are not mislabeled
as program savings, and administrative efficiency cannot become an unlimited
plug.

## Boundary

Ballots are package-construction inputs, not votes, public opinion, fiscal
scores, or law.
