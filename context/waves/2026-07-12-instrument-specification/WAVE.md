# Wave: Instrument Specification

## Objective

Turn the primary-balance challenger envelopes into two or three fully specified
policy variants suitable for an integrated score and public comparison.

## Initial variants

- progressive-heavy;
- broad shared-contribution; and
- primary-outlay-heavy.

Each must use the same target, baseline, window, household protections, service
floors, and scoring request.

## Pulses

| Pulse | Purpose | Status |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | Specify revenue variants and protections | complete |
| 02 | Specify primary-outlay variants and service floors | complete |
| 03 | Define joint scoring, uncertainty, and correction contract | active |
| 04 | Compare variants and reassess ballot readiness | pending |

## Decision gate

Can at least one variant be described precisely enough that an independent
scorer could estimate it without inventing material policy choices?
