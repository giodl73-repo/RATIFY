# Pulse 02: Primary-Outlay Specifications

## Result

Specified primary-outlay sides for all three variants and attached service,
rights, continuity, workforce, state, provider, and beneficiary floors.

## Role findings

- Minority Rights Guardian: Medicaid and Medicare changes require enforceable
  access, continuity, due-process, disability, and long-term-care floors.
- Election Administrator: program-level schedules and implementation ownership
  are still required; option-family names are not operational plans.
- Democratic Legitimacy: the outlay-heavy variant makes affected populations
  visible rather than presenting spending reductions as unallocated savings.
- Deliberation and Information: published CBO amounts remain useful magnitude
  references only; service floors must appear beside savings.
- Innovation Scout: run Voter Lab only after a shared score basis exists, or the
  simulation will reward arbitrary placeholder numbers.

## Decision

Advance to the joint scoring and uncertainty contract. Ballot readiness remains
blocked.
