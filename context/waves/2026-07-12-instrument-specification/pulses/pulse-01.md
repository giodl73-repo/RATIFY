# Pulse 01: Revenue Variant Specifications

## Result

Specified progressive-heavy, broad shared-contribution, and outlay-heavy
revenue sides using explicit CBO option variants, thresholds, rates, and bases.

## Role findings

- Comparative Evidence Steward: official CBO option definitions anchor each
  starting instrument; isolated scores remain nonadditive.
- Democratic Legitimacy: the variants expose materially different burden
  choices rather than presenting one technocratic package as inevitable.
- Minority Rights: VAT credits, emissions dividends and transition, and
  health-plan risk and geography protections are part of the policy, not notes.
- Deliberation and Information: three variants are sufficiently distinct for
  comparison; do not add more until these receive integrated estimates.
- Future Citizen: machine and reader records identify what is fixed and what a
  scorer must still parameterize.

## Decision

Advance to primary-outlay specifications. Revenue arithmetic is a scale check
only; ballot readiness remains blocked.
