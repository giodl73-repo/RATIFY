# Pulse 03: Joint Score and Correction Contract

## Result

Defined one annual 2026–2036 score contract for all variants and the complete
current-law No case. It requires fiscal, interaction, distribution, service,
rights, implementation, uncertainty, and debt outputs.

## Correction rule

Every variant must specify a reserve, annual reconciliation, a small
voter-approved automatic corridor, legislative correction outside that
corridor, early public review, and nonwaivable rights and continuity floors.

## Voter Lab decision

Continue simulation infrastructure and framing tests. Block substantive package
preference comparisons until a joint score exists or every missing input is
varied through an explicit synthetic sensitivity matrix. Placeholder certainty
would make a more sophisticated voter model less honest, not more realistic.

## Role findings

- Simulation Auditor: pass; model-input readiness is now an explicit gate.
- Comparative Evidence Steward: common baseline and window prevent cross-score
  arithmetic laundering.
- Minority Rights Guardian: automatic correction cannot cross rights or
  continuity floors.
- Future Citizen: current law is scored as the complete No case, allowing later
  voters to understand both choices.

## Decision

Advance to variant comparison and ballot-readiness reassessment.
