# Pulse 04: Variant Comparison and Wave Close

## Decision-gate answer

No variant is yet precise enough for an independent scorer to estimate without
inventing a material policy choice.

The broad shared-contribution variant is closest because it has the smallest
set of large levers and the clearest tradeoff. It remains blocked on the VAT
credit, emissions dividend and transition, and defense capability schedule.

## Completed

- Three explicit revenue specifications.
- Three primary-outlay specifications.
- Binding household, service, rights, continuity, and correction requirements.
- Common annual joint-score and current-law comparison contract.
- Explicit Voter Lab input-readiness gate.

## Retained lesson

Greater simulation detail cannot repair missing policy detail. Agent-based
models may explore uncertainty but must not hide unspecified laws inside voter
utility parameters.

## Next wave

Make the broad shared-contribution variant score-ready, while Voter Lab remains
a cross-cutting program developing reusable behavioral modules and calibration.
