# Pulse 03: Defense Capability and Workforce Path

## Result

Specified a 2027-start defense fiscal path, a proportional-smaller-force
implementation, workforce protections, public capability tests, and a closed
failure rule.

## Important correction

CBO 60920 is a budget option, not a defense strategy. Its $959 billion outlay
estimate starts in 2026 and does not reflect a particular force alternative.
The package no longer treats that number as portable. It claims no defense
savings until the chosen force path, transition costs, and 2027–2036 window are
independently scored.

## Decision

The provisional implementation preserves the force's composition by major unit
type while moving toward approximately 17% fewer active-component personnel and
associated resources by 2034. This is transparent and scoreable but remains
subject to capability review. Failure requires an explicit revised force plan,
replacement savings, or revenue before voters decide.
