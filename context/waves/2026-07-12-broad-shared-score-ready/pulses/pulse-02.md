# Pulse 02: Emissions Dividend and Transition

## Result

Specified the emissions-tax rate and trajectory, a 30% household dividend, a
10% formula-based worker and community transition, and a 60% primary-balance
contribution.

## Role findings

- Minority Rights Guardian: dividend, energy-affordability support, and tribal,
  territorial, rural, and isolated-community provisions are integral.
- Democratic Legitimacy: receipt allocation is fixed before scoring and cannot
  be redirected after the vote without the correction process.
- Anti-capture review: transition uses formula inputs, beneficial-ownership and
  performance disclosure, and no privileged political standing for recipients.
- Simulation Auditor: Voter Lab must use the net tax-dividend-transition package,
  not a gross carbon-price label.

## Decision

Advance to defense capability and workforce specification. The revenue envelope
will probably show a gap after VAT and emissions protections; retain it for the
joint score rather than filling it with assumptions.
