# Pulse 01: VAT Credit and Delivery

## Result

Specified the household consumption protection credit completely enough for an
independent score: amount, formula, poverty-guideline phaseout, eligibility,
tax unit, indexing, monthly delivery, nonfiler access, reconciliation,
benefit treatment, garnishment, administration, and reporting.

## Role findings

- Minority Rights Guardian: monthly delivery and nonfiler access are necessary
  because a later annual refund does not protect current consumption.
- Election Administrator analogue: Treasury and IRS need a long startup window,
  multiple enrollment channels, correction, appeal, and public performance data.
- Democratic Legitimacy: the credit formula is fixed before the score; it cannot
  be shrunk after revenue estimates simply to make the package fit.
- Simulation Auditor: future Voter Lab incidence inputs must use net VAT plus
  credit, not gross VAT labels.

## Decision

Advance to the emissions dividend and regional transition specification. Net
VAT revenue is unknown until rescored.
