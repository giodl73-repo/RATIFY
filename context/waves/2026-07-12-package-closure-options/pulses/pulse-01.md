# Pulse 01: Closure Arithmetic and Invariants

## Invariants

- Treat every result as synthetic sensitivity until jointly scored.

  Yes challenger / No current law.

## Closure arithmetic

The central synthetic shortfall is $1.640T. Linear scaling of the current net
VAT result implies an 11.5% narrow VAT, not a small adjustment to 5%. A
progressive alternative adds a 3-point high-threshold AGI surtax and full-rate
current taxation of foreign corporate income. The third alternative retains
the policy package and changes the fiscal target from primary balance to the
partial path.
