# Pulse 04: Human-Test Interface Selection

## Decision

Select the persistent difference strip plus common-facts refresh for
preregistered human testing. Retain the sequential layered card as control.

Do not treat the V2 pass as deployment evidence. The selected design must still
meet the external protocol's fact-level, subgroup, order, false-certainty, and
accessibility thresholds with human participants.

Equal official wording is a nonnegotiable control. Candidate-favorable wording
produces a modeled comprehension disadvantage even when layout is symmetric.
