# Wave: Joint-Score Sensitivity

## Objective

Build a reproducible pre-score sensitivity matrix for the broad/shared package
that varies every material missing fiscal value and reports whether the package
is plausibly close to the primary-balance target.

## Evidence boundary

This is a synthetic diagnostic, not a CBO, JCT, agency, or independent score.
Published component estimates anchor scale only. Modified policy, window,
interaction, protection, and implementation effects remain assumptions.

## Decision gate

Across disclosed lower, central, and higher fiscal-effect assumptions, is the
broad/shared package close enough to commission for formal scoring unchanged,
or does it require an enumerated closure instrument first?

**Decision:** It requires an enumerated closure instrument first. Every tested
case misses the target; the gap ranges from $527 billion to $2.600 trillion and
is $1.640 trillion in the central synthetic case.
