# Pulse 01: Assumption Registry

## Result

Created a machine-readable registry for all six broad/shared fiscal components.
Every factor is visibly synthetic and separated from the published CBO scale
anchor. No administrative savings, interest dividend, macroeconomic feedback,
or unpriced 2035–2036 defense savings closes the package.

## Limitation

The ranges are deliberate stress assumptions, not empirically calibrated
confidence intervals. They diagnose structural distance; they do not substitute
for a joint score.
