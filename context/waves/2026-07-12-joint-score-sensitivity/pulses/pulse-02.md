# Pulse 02: Gap Decomposition

## Result

The synthetic matrix produces:

| Case | Adjustment | Target covered | Gap |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Lower fiscal effect | $2.625T | 50.2% | $2.600T |
| Central synthetic | $3.585T | 68.6% | $1.640T |
| Higher fiscal effect | $4.698T | 89.9% | $0.527T |

The intended 73/27 mix also does not survive mechanically. Realized revenue
shares range from 64.1% to 70.6% because household protections reduce the
revenue side and defense/Medicare protections reduce outlay savings differently.

## Interpretation

The package is structurally short in every tested case. Formal scoring remains
necessary, but commissioning the current package as though only precision were
missing would misstate the design problem.
