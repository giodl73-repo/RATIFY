# Wave: Human Comprehension Test Instrument

## Objective

Turn the selected persistent comparison and sequential control into a
preregisterable human-study instrument with exact randomization, questions,
scoring, accessibility checks, analysis code, and a synthetic dry run.

## Pulses

| Pulse | Purpose | Status |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | Freeze stimuli and question wording | active |
| 02 | Specify assignment, consent, privacy, and accessibility | pending |
| 03 | Implement scoring and synthetic dry run | pending |
| 04 | Audit preregistration package and external dependencies | pending |

## Decision gate

Could an independent research team administer and analyze the study without
choosing outcomes, exclusions, thresholds, or favorable wording after seeing
results?
